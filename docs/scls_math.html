SCLS Math "Carl"

Mathematics can be interpreted as the root of everything in this universe. To use it more easily in C++, you can use our SCLS Math "Carl" library.

Presentation

What is SCLS Math "Carl" ?

As everything in the universe, SCLS needs mathematics to be usable. For that, SCLS uses a special part of the library : SCLS Math "Carl". It is named after a mathematician, known as the father of modern mathematics : Carl Friedrich Gauss. This library does not pretend to replace the include of "math" in your code, but rather it is an extension of it.
